Terms & Conditions

The trip in website are operated by Plan Nepal Travel & Tours and We have tried to make the booking terms and conditions with us as simple as possible. The following booking conditions apply to trips.

Booking

We prefer to have the confirmed booking in advance prior to the arrival to Nepal, Pre-booking gives us the enough time to organize your trip well in Nepal, Tibet, Bhutan & India.

Payment

Payment For Trekking and Tours: At all the trips you will book with Plan Nepal Travel need 20% of the total contracted price to confirm your spot for the trip. And the rest amount can be settle upon arrival upon arrival in Kathmandu by USD, Euro, GBP cash or Visa or Master card.

Cancellation Policy:

If we fail to provide you the trip (completely or partially), under unexpected situations which is not in our control. ( landslides, flight cancellation, natural disaster) we would offer you alternative trip of similar interest and standard. If that doesn't fit your interest, we will refund you the money deducting expenses till that point of the trip, but there is no compensation policy.
