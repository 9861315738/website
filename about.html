ABOUT US

Tenzo Hotel is a place to enjoy above the clouds, dense forests, beautiful view of sunset and panoramic view of entire Kathmandu valley at the same time.

The resort is spanned to more than 8000 sq ft. build with modern architecture. Each room has been designed in a way that reflects the serene view of Kathmandu valley. The resort provides daily housekeeping and evening turndown services, accommodates different group size. It has world-class facilities for organizing meeting. At the resort, you could be torn between staying indoors and going outdoors. If you are the outdoor types, the resort can arrange many activities such as horse riding, city tour, nature walks, jungle safari, mountain biking and trekking.

There are several things you would like to view and feel while your stay in Nepal that you can experience at Ratnagiri resort. The interiors, the food, entertainment and mountain view inside one roof of the Ratnagiri Resort, set amidst the thick forest, helps you rediscover yourself. The serene mountain landscape of the land of gods and the unique architecture of Ratnagiri is so captivating that you may not want to go back to the city and its chores.
