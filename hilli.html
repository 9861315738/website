Annapurna Base Camp Heli Tour

Annapurna Base Camp Heli Tour is one of the fastest and easiest way to reach base camp and enjoy mountain in all its glory. On the way to Mount Annapurna Base camp we explore aerial view of world's top 8th thousand meter Himalayan range of Mountains that include Mt. Fistail, Mt. Annapurna I and II and Mt. Dhaulagiri. These tour is ideal for all age group. However, these Heli Tour is perfect for those visitors who are not physically fitness and timely unable to trek in Annapurna Region. These tour start with heli ride from kathmandu or Pokhara airport and fly to nortnern himalayan range heading east to Annapurna Base Camp. You will fly through beautiful local village, diverse landscapes and view of city with different lakes. You will be given around 30 minutes ground time at Annapurna Base Camp. It also provide photogrpaphic opportunities and also a decent chance to take your feet into base camp.
